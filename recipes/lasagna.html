Lasagne Verdi alla Bolognese Recipe

Ingredients (10 Servings)

Ragu:

  • 1 (3 ounce) package pancetta, minced
  • ½ cup diced carrot
  • ½ cup diced celery
  • ½ cup diced yellow onion
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 7 ounces ground beef
  • 6 ounces ground pork
  • ½ cup dry red wine
  • 1 ½ cups plain tomato sauce
  • 2 cups vegetable broth, or more as needed

Pasta:

  • ¼ cup water
  • 1 pinch salt
  • ½ (8 ounce) package frozen spinach
  • ½ (16 ounce) package gluten-free all-purpose baking flour
  • 2 medium eggs, at room temperature
  • salt to taste
  • ½ cup whole milk

Bechamel Sauce:

  • ¼ cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoons unsalted butter
  • ¼ cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoons gluten-free all-purpose baking flour
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cups finely grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese
